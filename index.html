About

Welcome to my portfolio! Here I have presented all the assignments and projects I have worked on while making use of my knowledge in the ICT field. The design is mainly inspired by the simplicity and minimalism of high fashion websites while also taking into account the actual needs and important aspects of a professional portfolio. For the development, I used different techniques, animations, mockups, version control and skills which I have learned from both university and personal research.

Here, on the main page, you can find the four different modules while each of them takes you to a page, where you can select and read about the specific project/assignment, related to the module.

Featured Module

User Centered Design

In the User Centered Design module, I learn how to research, test, and design interactive media products that meet the needs of my clients and users. The primary learning outcome is to create prototypes of interactive media products using iterations, user research, and examples from the professional field. Prototypes are designed to be user-friendly with techniques ranging from low-fidelity paper sketches to high-fidelity using tools like Figma.
