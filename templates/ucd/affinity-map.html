Affinity map

This is the affinity map which me and two more classmates made in order to visualize and organize information from a brainstorming session. It is about a persona - Angela, who is a student too tired to cook after university and decides to order a pizza from a famous food delivery app - Uber Eats.

Our thoughts are divided into four different categories - her customer journey, questions and comments related to her experience with the app, and ideas for improvement.

Based on the affinity map, we also made a Customer journey map where you can see the persona's mood during her journey with more specific categories like user tasks and painpoints.
