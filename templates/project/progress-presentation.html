Progress presentation

This presentation concluded our group's used research methods and their results, the brand guideline, moodboard, low-fidelity prototype and timeline.

We introduced our audience to the persona, survey answers and their useful contribution to the design process. We also presented our logo, colour palette and ideas through the brand guideline.

As for me, I handled the final part of the presentation about which you can read more below ↓

Timeline and next steps

I talked and presented the low-fidelity prototype, showing the initial structure and layout of the website, based on results from the research methods. Then, I introduced the audience to our timeline at the moment, explaining how we are a little bit ahead of schedule, because of the prototype, allowing us to focus more on the next tasks.

Lastly, I concluded the presentation by describing our next steps, which are to first analyse the research results and update the prototype accordingly, to design the high-fidelity prototype and implement specific colours, fonts, final website structure and to finally, divide the tasks related to coding, and build the website itself.
