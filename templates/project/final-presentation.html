Final tasks

As the lead developer of the team, the last sprint of our group project was my time to shine. We meticulously assigned final tasks to each team member based on their strongest skills and abilities. My contribution focused on "bringing the prototypes and designs to life" by enhancing them with the necessary functionality and assisting with design decisions.

Final process

In terms of the design aspect, I reached a point where I couldn't tell the differnce between the Figma prototype and the actual website, indicating its completion. Moving forward, gathering user feedback became crucial in finding the right balance between functionality and avoiding cluttered animations. I had discussions with both my team and teachers, and upon receiving their approval, I proceeded to implement links, functional elements, and animations. Furthermore, to ensure that everything is ready, I conducted a final usability testing interview.

Finally, I will briefly metion the final presentation. We encountered challenges related to the availability of group members, but we successfully managed to overcome them. Overall, I am very satisfied with the results, particularly considering the tremendous effort invested to accomplish everything. I am pleased to say that my communication skills have significantly improved throughout this semester, establishing a solid foundation for my future in the professional field.
