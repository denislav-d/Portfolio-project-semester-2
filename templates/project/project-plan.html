Project plan

The project plan was our first milestone in the project timeline. We used different research methods to find the answers to the research questions which helped us define the actual problem and gave us an idea of how we are going to solve it. Next, we made our risk assessment by creating different scenarios about possible risks, which still helps us to not let them happen.

Finally, we set up our milestones for the whole project which are represented by the Gantt chart. A timeline with every major progression (documentation, research, creating a prototype and implementation) supports our time-management and work that we must do.

Project plan presentation

In our presentation we concluded the most important information from the project plan. The content was divided into different sections - our approach, goals, empathy map, team conditions, risk assessment and communication. We clearly defined our goals, scope and were able to show our real progress with the Gantt chart. We were also marked as ambitious for trying out the scrum approach.
