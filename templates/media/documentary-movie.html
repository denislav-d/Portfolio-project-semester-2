Recording the movie

Once storyboarding was completed, we proceeded to the filming stage. I assembled the actors while others gathered the necessary equipment and devices. Together, we headed to a specific location in the R10 building that was suitable for our initial scenes. At first, we encountered several audio issues, experimenting with a camera-attached microphone. However, we eventually switched to a lavalier microphone that could be attached to the performers. It took some time to understand the connections and operations, but we successfully managed to figure it out and resumed shooting the scenes.

In conclusion, we remained at the university until its closing time, which proved to be worthwhile as we managed to capture every scene we required, except for one. My primary responsibility revolved around the actual filming, as I had only one minor background role. One particular challenge I encountered was maintaining a steady camera and executing smooth movements, particularly during following shots.

Editing the content

Additionally, I took charge of the editing process, starting with importing the raw footage from the memory card and arranging each scene in chronological order on Premiere Pro, following our story timeline. I proceeded by trimming all the clips, eliminating the initial sounds that served as signal for the actors at the beginning of recording. Furthermore, I incorporated transitions between scenes, along with other effects, including a split-screen scene.

Subsequently, I undertook the task of creating the credits and the final part of the movie, which proved to be more challenging than anticipated. Specifically, we needed to incorporate a typewriter effect, which unfortunately was not available in this software. To overcome this limitation, I searched for alternative methods and discovered that viable options were scarce and not very practical. As a workaround, I resorted to screen recording Instagram effect with a green screen for each clip, which were then edited, rotated, trimmed, and scaled accordingly. All in all, this movie project presented a completely new experience for me, enhancing my skills in both filming and editing, while also providing valuable insight into the producer's mindset.
