Design week poster

This poster is my second attempt of using something more creative for the background. Similar to the Fashion poster which I made, here I also used a random image which I took in Maastricht. Though, this time the initial colors of the photo were more important since there is no gradient map used.

The idea came from browsing interesting techniques in photoshop, specifically 3D effects and the inspiration from design weeks, since I did attend the one in Eindhoven and really enjoyed it.

Deeper explanation of the process below ↓

Process of design

First of all, I started by creating a New Mesh from Layer with a Depth Map to Solid Extrusion. While there are not many additional effects to apply, the part in which you just rotate the object in many different ways until you like it takes the longest time.

Then, I added saturation to the colors of the object to make them more vibrant and eye-catching.

Finishing the poster was quite easy by adding the text and creating the little filter for the clipping word on the bottom.
