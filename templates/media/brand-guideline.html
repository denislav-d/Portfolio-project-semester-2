Brand guideline

This is our group's brand guideline which consists of clearly defined rules and standards that communicate how our brand should be represented to the world. It helps clients ensure consistency and demonstrate what the brand is, what it does, and what it stands for.

The brand guideline includes two main sections - Brand vision, representing our goal, concept, target group and values, and Brand identity, which shows the colour palette, logo usage (appearance, visibility, patterns) and the logo concept itself.

Process of design

The process first begun by writing down the most important aspects which were needed in the brand guideline. Although the resources were limited to only the logo and colours on the brand identity side, since there is no real company and brand, there was a lot to note about the brand's recognition.

By dividing everything into the two sections, mentioned in the beginning, we decided to go with a minimalistic approach for each slide in order to match the website's design. Every main point is described simply and easily-understandable. As you can see, we also made sure to make use of the brand's colour palette.
