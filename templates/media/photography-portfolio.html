Photography portfolio

The photography portfolio was one of the assignments which I really enjoyed doing. Walking around in the beautiful, sunny (if you are lucky) weather and taking pictures of the world around you. I have put effort into taking a little bit different and more creative approach to make it more pleasant for the reader.

For the background, I decided to use a pastel colour close to white at first glance, but actually leaning towards mint. On top of that, there are contrasting dark text and pictures with a black border.
