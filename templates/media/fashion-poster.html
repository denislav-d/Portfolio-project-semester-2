Fashion week poster

As strange as it may sound, this modern fashion week invitation poster was made from a random photo of the Fontys R10 building which I took while we were outdoors, having the assignment to go around and take photos in order to explore and experiment with different color palettes.

The initial inspiration was from this type of liquified background which I have seen in many places before and wondered how it is made. After doing a quick research, I found that the ability to create the pattern is only a couple clicks away. I really enjoyed the process of making the background since it gave me the option to input my own creativity and not just simply copy it.

Deeper explanation of the process below ↓

Process of design

I first started by creating a Gradient Map which allowed me to pick different colors for the start, flowing and end of the gradient. Then by using the Wind filter, I created the distorted effect. Next, I played around for a while with the Liquify filter to achieve this twisted look.

Of course, at the end I placed the different sized and rotated text using my favourite font - Helvetica Neue, setting the theme of the poster to be fashion.
