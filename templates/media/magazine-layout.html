Magazine layout

This is the finished product for our magazine layout assignment. The task was not just a simple layout, but to follow different design principles and requirements. Each page had to have a title, an article, covering 1/3 of the page with margins, and an ad, covering 1/5 of the space.

As you can see, we decided to go for a more creative approach and positioned the main elements in some unusual places. We made the first title sideways and included a quote next to it. The idea is to have a nice section hierarchy which makes the user first read the title and then the paragraphs.

Deeper explanation of the process below ↓

Process of design

First of all, we started by doing some sketches, while mainly focusing on the positioning, to get the idea of a good-looking and accessible page.

Then, we combined the best of our ideas into one and started by placing the 2 large images, which purposely overlap. After that, we placed the ads and titles, considering the place left for the articles.

Finally, we did the text paragraphs by using justified alignment as that is the proper way for magazines.
