Personal logo

This is my personal logo, designed in Adobe Illustrator. Therefore, it is exported in a vector format. I included it on my website as a .svg, which gives the following benefits: high resolution, small file size, infinite scalability and transparent background support.

The logo mockup is on this current page itself, showing how the logo is supposed to be used in the center of the header.

I wanted to make a simple and luxurious logo, which immediately led me into researching some high fashion brands and their logos, since I have a big passion and interest in the fashion industry.

Deeper explanation of the process below ↓

Process of design

Firstly, I took into account that both of my names start with the same letter. My initial idea was something similar to the brand “Dsquared2” – the name speaks for itself, but their case is a little bit different - the names of the brothers which are the brand's founders both start with “D”.

Of course, I wanted something more original and I didn’t really like how the letter “D” looked while standing alone in a sans-serif font. Therefore, I started thinking about using a serif or cursive font and my first thought was a brand which I really like – “Loewe”. I did a deeper research and their logo really has a great history (too long to explain here). Taking a quick glance at the logo, it is an anagram with a beautiful letter “L”, repeating in different ways – mirrored and flipped.

This was my main inspiration and I started experimenting. One of the first attempts was also an anagram, but I didn’t really adore it and wanted something more original. My next idea was to figure out a good connection between two of the letters, which turned out great, as you can see from the iterations below. I also tried rotating the logo in different ways to see if it looked better, but decided to stick with the one without rotation.
